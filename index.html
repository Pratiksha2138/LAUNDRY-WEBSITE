Development of Responsive Web / Mobile App for Laundry Service Provider

Develop a platform to assist Laundry Service Provider to effectively manage his/her business online giving rich digital experience to his/her customers.

Using a developed web/mobile app, a customer who needs Laundry services shall be able to place an order for laundry service at the same time Laundry Service Provider shall be able to manage all customer requests for Laundry Service.

Features to be developed for a working prototype:

  • Register and Login Feature for customer
    • All required customer fields with data validation
  • Admin dashboard for Laundry Service Provider
    • List of all customer requests (Pending, Completed)
    • Payment received/pending from each customer
    • List of customer scheduled pick-up and delivery time
    • List of Customer complaints and queries
  • Customer dashboard
    • New request for Laundry Service with scheduled pick-up and delivery time
    • List of past requests with status
    • Send complaint or query to Laundry Service provider
    • Payment mode (online payment / cash on delivery)

Any other feature/s focused around web/mobile app's usability (functional and non functional), ability to provide rich user experience can be added.
